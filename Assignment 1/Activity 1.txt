... This program will help you make a meatball sandwich
Collect required ingredients and tools:
  -Pre-sliced Italian roll
  -Cooked meatballs
  -Pepperoni
  -Italian Seasoning 
  -Ladle 
  -Plate
  -Air fryer
  -Measuring cup
  -tablespoon measuring spoon. 
  -Pepperjack cheese 
  -Sauce
  -Pot
  -Stove
 ## Prepare meatballs
Open meatball package.
Place 6 meatballs into pot.
Close meatball package.
Open sauce bottle. 
Pour sauce into measuring cup until 1 cup line is met. 
Pour 1 cup of sauce into pot.
Close sauce bottle.
Apply low flame to pot for 15 minutes. 
Remove heat from pot. 
Grab the Italian roll and open 180 degrees. 
Set bread on plate.
Grab ladle.
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place on the left side of Italian roll. 
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place one inch from the left end of Italian roll. 
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place two inchs from the left end of Italian roll. 
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place three inchs from the left end of Italian roll. 
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place four inchs from the left end of Italian roll. 
Using ladle, scoop one meatball out of pot and place five inchs from the left end of Italian roll. 
Set ladle down.
Open pepperoni package. 
Grab six pepperonis and place on top of meatballs.
Close pepperoni package. 
Grab measuring cup.
Open cheese container. 
Add cheese to measuring cup until quarter cup line in met. 
Grab quarter cup shredded cheese.
Spread evenly across sandwich. 
Close cheese package.
Open Air fry er.
Place sandwich in air fryer.
Close air fryer and set to two minutes 400 degrees.
After air fryer is complete, open air fryer.
Remove sandwich and place on plate. 
Open italian seasoning container. 
Measure 1 tbsp of seasoning.
Spread seasoning evenly accross sandwich. 
Close seasoning container. 
Close air fyrer
Close bread ninety degrees and enjoy.
