This program takes inputted age in years and convert to months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. 

Input
  "What is your age in years?" (.25 = X and 3 months old, .50 = X and 6 months old, .75 = x and 9 months old) (Years)
  
Processing 
  Convert years to months x12
  Convert years to days x365.25
  Convert days to hours x24
  Convert hours to minutes x60
  Convert minutes to seconds x60 
  
Output
  "Being x years old you are: x months old, x days old, x hours old, x minutes old, and x seconds old. PHEW!"
